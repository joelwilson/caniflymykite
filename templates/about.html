What

Can I Fly my Kite is a tool for kite fliers to quickly and easily determine if it is "weathery" enough outside to fly. Nothing else is as easy, painless, and to the point!

How

We are state-of-the-art: Weather and location data comes straight from Wunderground.com. Everything is pieced together with vanilla HTML and CSS and backed up with a Python core.
