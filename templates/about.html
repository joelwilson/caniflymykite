What

Can I Kly my Kite is a tool for kite fliers to quickly and easily determine if it is "weathery" enough outside to fly. Nothing else is as easy, painless, and to the point!

How

We are state-of-the-art! Weather data comes straight from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration. Location info is pulled from Geonames. The whole thing is pieced together with vanilla HTML and CSS and backed up with a Python core.
